Meet Finn the Fennec Fox!

Hello there! I'm Finn, a lively and curious fennec fox living at the Wildlife Sanctuary. Born in the heart of the Sahara Desert, I was rescued by the kind folks at the sanctuary when I was just a pup. My large ears help me stay cool in the desert heat and hear the faintest sounds of potential friends or foes.

I love to dig burrows and explore my surroundings with my sharp senses. My favorite activities include playing hide-and-seek with my caretakers and nibbling on delicious treats like insects and fruits. I'm known for my playful antics and energetic personality, making me a favorite among visitors.
